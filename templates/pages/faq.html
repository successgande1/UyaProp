Frequently Asked Questions

Select one of the frequently asked questions below to learn more UyaProp Property Management.

Question about UyaProp

What is UyaProp?

UyaProp Web Application is an online platform designed to connect property owners (landlords and agents) with potential tenants or buyers for FREE.

It allows property owners to list their properties for free and provides a user-friendly interface for prospects to search and find suitable properties.

How can I list my property on your platform?

To list your property, simply sign up as a landlord, agent or Prospect on our website.

Once you've created an account, you can easily add property details, including descriptions, images, amenities, and contact information.

Our platform will guide you through the process, making it quick and straightforward.

Is it really free to list my property?

Yes, listing your property on our platform is absolutely free. We believe in providing a transparent and accessible platform for property owners to showcase their properties without any upfront costs.

Question about renting

How do prospects search for properties?

Prospects can search for properties on our platform using various criteria, such as location, property type, price range, number of bedrooms, and more.

Our advanced search filters help narrow down the options to find the most relevant listings.

Can I communicate directly with prospects who are interested in my property?

Yes, we facilitate direct communication between property owners/agents and prospects. When a prospect shows interest in a property, they can reach out to you using the message feature provided in their account. You can then communicate directly to schedule viewings or answer any questions they may have.

Is my personal contact information visible to everyone?

Your personal contact information is not publicly displayed on the website. Instead, prospects can reach out to you using a messaging feature within our platform. This ensures your privacy while still allowing effective communication.

Can I upload multiple properties?

Absolutely! Whether you're a landlord or an agent, you can upload multiple properties to our platform. Our intuitive interface allows you to manage all your listings conveniently from a single account.

How long does a property listing remain active?

By default, a property listing remains active on our platform for a specified duration, usually 30 days. However, you have the option to extend the listing period or mark it as rented or sold once it's no longer available.

Do you offer any additional services for landlords or agents?

Along with free property listings, we provide additional services, such as analytics and insights, to help landlords and agents make informed decisions about their properties. These services may vary, so please check our website or contact our support team for more details.

How can I get support if I encounter any issues?

We have a dedicated support team ready to assist you with any questions or issues you may encounter. You can reach out to our support team through the contact form or information provided on our website, and we'll be more than happy to assist you.
